# NAVLYS — Payment FAQ

> Public page. 15 questions, plain image-rich language.

## 1. How can I pay?
Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and SEPA bank transfer for annual. Everything runs through Stripe, the global standard. Your bank info never touches our servers.

## 2. Is the payment secure?
Yes. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified (the highest). Every card is authenticated via 3D Secure 2 (SMS or your bank's app notification). NAVLYS stores no banking data.

## 3. What's the difference between Monthly and Annual?
Monthly: 49 €/month, leave anytime. Annual: 490 €/year, save 98 € **and** a real NAVLYS bronze coin, hand-struck, shipped to your home.

## 4. Is the bronze coin actually bronze?
Yes. Real CuSn8 (92% copper, 8% tin), 32 mm, 12 g, hand-struck in a European workshop, numbered series. Navy velvet box, signed card.

## 5. When do I receive the coin?
3 to 5 weeks after annual payment. Confirmation email at order time, then a second email with UPS tracking when shipped.

## 6. What if I don't receive the coin?
UPS tracks every parcel with declared value 50 €. If lost or damaged, we re-strike one and ship free of charge.

## 7. What if I want a refund?
You have 14 days after payment to request a full refund (EU right of withdrawal). Beyond that, case by case. If the bronze coin has already shipped, it's not taken back.

## 8. Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes anytime from your member area ("Billing"). Access stays active until the end of the paid period. No cancellation fees.

## 9. Which VAT applies?
French VAT 20% for FR private individuals. EU professionals with valid VAT number: reverse charge (0%). Non-EU: no VAT.

## 10. How does the VIBEZ code work?
It's our promo ladder: over 3 weeks, the longer you wait, the smaller the discount. W1: -80% monthly / -50% annual. W2: -70% / -30%. W3: -10% on everything. Each passing week is a missed wave.

## 11. Can I change my plan mid-way?
Yes. From "Billing", switch monthly → annual (prorated) or vice versa (at next renewal). If you switch monthly → annual after signup, you'll receive the bronze coin.

## 12. Is the payment recurring automatically?
Yes for Monthly and Annual. Stripe re-charges on the anniversary date. You always get an email 7 days before the annual renewal. Bio Live is one-time: no recurring charge.

## 13. My payment failed, what now?
Stripe automatically retries for 3 weeks. You can also update your card from "Billing". Your access stays active during the retry window.

## 14. Will I get an invoice?
Yes, automatic by email after each payment (PDF, VAT line, business name if pro). Re-downloadable from your member area.

## 15. Who do I contact for payment issues?
Direct email: `support@navlys.com`. Reply within 24 business hours. For bronze coin: `fulfillment@navlys.com`.
