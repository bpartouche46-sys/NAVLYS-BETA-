# Bank brief · BNP Paribas (EN)

## Average margin per retail client
- ~ €380 / year for a multi-product client (account + savings + life insurance + mortgage)

## Opaque products
- BNP Cardif multi-unit life insurance: ~1.0% UC management fee + entry fees
- House funds over-represented in mandate-managed portfolios
- Mortgage: arbitrage margin + group borrower insurance

## Client-side improvement axes
1. Renegotiate borrower insurance (Lemoine law, FR)
2. Externalize life insurance to an independent player
3. Audit account-keeping fees and unused paid services

## NAVLYS counter-strategy
- 360° private audit: identify the 3 biggest leak points
- Migration opaque products → open architecture
- Quarterly wealth dashboard
