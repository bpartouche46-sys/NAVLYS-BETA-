## 1. The idea, simple

People don't know how much their bank "keeps for itself" each year.
NAVLYS offers a free calculator: type your numbers, see the average.
**It's information, not advice.**

## 2. What the calculator does

You enter:
- What you have in your current account
- What you have in your regulated savings account
- What you have in your classic life insurance
- Outstanding mortgage capital
- Your primary bank

The calculator shows, on average:
- How much your bank keeps each year, roughly
- How much over 10 years
- Comparison to the French average
- Button: "See how NAVLYS helps me plan" → homepage simulator

## 3. What NAVLYS does NOT do here

- ❌ No personalized advice
- ❌ No private audit
- ❌ No replacement product
- ❌ No appointment
