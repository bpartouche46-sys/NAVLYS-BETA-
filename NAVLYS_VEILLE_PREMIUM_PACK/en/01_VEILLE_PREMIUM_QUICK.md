# NAVLYS Premium World Watch — English quick pack

> All voices do not weigh the same. The engine listens only to those whose words move markets within hours.

---

## The three circles of oracles

**Circle 1 — Deciding institutions** (T+0 minutes)
Central banks, regulators, governments. Their word is a decision applied immediately.

**Circle 2 — Official press agencies** (T+0 to T+1h)
Reuters, Bloomberg, AP, AFP, Dow Jones Newswires, Xinhua. If 4 out of 6 cover a story within the hour, it goes global within six.

**Circle 3 — Individual voices with proven influence** (T+0 to T+24h)
CEOs of mega-corporations, top economists, individuals whose every public statement moves the needle (Powell, Lagarde, Yellen, Buffett, Dimon, Fink, Musk).

---

## Inclusion criteria (3 of 4 required)

1. **Systematic uptake**: statements covered by ≥4 of the 6 official agencies within the hour.
2. **Measurable market correlation**: last 10 major statements followed by a net move (>0.3%) on a world index within 24h.
3. **Institutional or capital status**: speaks for an institution or capital that actually weighs.
4. **Public traceability**: timestamped, public official channels.

## Quorum rule

- 6/6 in 30 min → **major event** (95-100/100)
- 5/6 in 60 min → **strong event** (75-94/100)
- 4/6 in 60 min → **notable event** (50-74/100)
- ≤3/6 → **noise** (<50, ignored)

---

## Four temporal windows

| Window | Duration | What we look at |
|---|---|---|
| T+15 min | immediate shock | majors indexes + currencies |
| T+1 h | absorption | confirmation or revert |
| T+4 h | propagation | spread to other markets |
| T+24 h | digestion | session close, next-day effect |

## Oracle classes

- **Class A** (score >0.8) → quasi-systematic effect
- **Class B** (0.5-0.8) → frequent effect
- **Class C** (0.3-0.5) → occasional
- **<0.3** → out of the list (no weight)
