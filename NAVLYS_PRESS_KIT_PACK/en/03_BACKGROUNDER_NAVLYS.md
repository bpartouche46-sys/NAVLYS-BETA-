# NAVLYS BACKGROUNDER

## Who is NAVLYS?

NAVLYS is a French educational brand launched in 2026, dedicated to financial literacy for retail savers. It was founded by **Bruno Mark Partouche**, a French Investment Adviser (CIF) registered with ORIAS, based in Marseille on the Mediterranean coast.

The NAVLYS name evokes **navigation** — the brand borrows from maritime vocabulary: bearing, sap, drift, fair wind. The metaphor is deliberate: personal finance, like the sea, requires a clear bearing, a solid hull, and patience.

## Mission

To give French households a clear bearing on their money — without selling a product, without promising returns.

NAVLYS attacks three blind spots:
1. **The unreadability of fees** — most savers don't know how much they pay.
2. **The lack of a bearing** — between savings books, euro funds, ETFs, REITs, crypto, many savers no longer know what to do.
3. **Constant financial noise** — social media, finfluencers, alerts, FOMO: NAVLYS opposes scarcity to overload.

## The sap — the DNA

The "sap" of NAVLYS is what flows through everything the brand produces:
- **Independence**: no retrocession, no capital ties with any broker.
- **Pedagogy**: we explain, we don't sell.
- **Calm**: one bearing per day, not ten.
- **Honesty**: no return promises.
- **Patience**: the horizon, not the trade.

## The three tools

### 1. Portfolio allocation simulator
Free tool that helps users visualise a theoretical allocation based on horizon, risk tolerance and goals. No product is recommended by name. No data stored.

### 2. Hidden-fee calculator
Free tool that lets users input their contract characteristics (life insurance, PEA, brokerage account) and visualise total fees over 10, 20 and 30 years. Often a shock.

### 3. The "daily compass"
A single, daily, short, educational publication. Released each morning. One concept, one maritime metaphor, one actionable note. No push notification, no FOMO, no promo.
