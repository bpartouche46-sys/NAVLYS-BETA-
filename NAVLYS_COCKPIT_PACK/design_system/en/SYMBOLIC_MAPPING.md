## The mechanics of the day (in plain words)

1. Each **"new day"**, the **market wind** turns (bounded randomness).
2. The **Fortress** advances slowly, like a mainsail holding the heading (~5%/yr smoothed).
3. The **Active Play** takes what the day's wind gives it (gain or loss, bounded).
4. On a gain, the **Reallocation winch** decides what share goes to the vault (Fortress) and what stays lively (Active Play) — this is the engine of exponential growth.
5. The **Sail trim winch** says whether you push hard (aggressive) or shorten sail (cautious).
6. The **Allocation winch** redefines, at any time, the 90/10 split between vault and play.

All of it, one-handed, from your cockpit. *The wind changes. Your heading does not.*
