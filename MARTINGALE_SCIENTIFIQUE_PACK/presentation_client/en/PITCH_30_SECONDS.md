# 30-second pitch — NAVLYS (EN)

> Memorise it. Calm voice, steady pace, breathe between each sentence.

---

**(0 → 5 s) — The hook**

> "Picture your money as an island."

**(5 → 12 s) — The principle**

> "On that island, you build a fortress for 90 % of your savings. Nobody gets in. Not even you on a bad day."

**(12 → 20 s) — The sailboat**

> "Next to it, a small sailboat carries the remaining 10 %. When the sea is calm, it sails out and grows the money. When the sea turns rough, it returns to port."

**(20 → 27 s) — The proof**

> "Across ten thousand simulated trips, the system gained 17 % on average without ever sinking. And 7 tides out of 10 are favourable."

**(27 → 30 s) — The promise**

> "That is NAVLYS. Safety first. Performance after."

---

## Short variants

**10-second version** :
> "You protect 90 % in a fortress. You sail with 10 % on a boat. If the boat sinks, the fortress holds. That is NAVLYS."

**5-second version** :
> "90 % sheltered. 10 % at sail. Never the opposite."

---

## Delivery rules

1. Breathe between each sentence.
2. Lean on the words "fortress", "sailboat", "never".
3. Don't smile when saying "if the boat sinks". It is serious.
4. Smile when saying "that is NAVLYS".
