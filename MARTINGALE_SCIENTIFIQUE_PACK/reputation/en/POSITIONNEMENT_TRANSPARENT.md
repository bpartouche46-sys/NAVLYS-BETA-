## 1. Two entities, two roles

**NAVLYS** is a public-facing educational product. It is the app that shows the fortress and the sailboat. It is open to anyone, without conditions. It does not execute orders itself. It is not a discretionary management service.

**brunopartouche.com** is the personal hub of Bruno Mark Partouche. It is the space where regulated wealth advice takes place, under the CIF (Financial Investment Adviser) status, ORIAS registration pending, [entité — hors dépôt] mentoring.

These two entities are distinct yet bound by the same scientific rigour. The science that powers NAVLYS is validated, documented and made accessible behind brunopartouche.com.

---

## 2. Why the separation ?

Because French and European regulation clearly distinguish :

- **Financial education** (open to any audience without personalised-advice obligations).
- **Investment advice** (requires regulated registration, a service agreement, a risk-profile assessment, documentary traceability).

NAVLYS stays on the education side. brunopartouche.com handles the advisory side for those who request it.

This separation protects the public : you can learn freely on NAVLYS with no commitment. If you want to move to a personalised application, brunopartouche.com offers the regulated framework.
