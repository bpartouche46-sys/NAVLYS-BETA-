# 📜 ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION — NAVLYS WEB SERVICE LTD

> Draft prepared 2026-07-08 for filing with the Israeli Registrar of Companies
> (רשם החברות). Positioning: **SaaS / high-tech enterprise**, the prerequisite
> for Israel's preferred-tax regimes (Encouragement of Capital Investments Law).
> The binding Hebrew version (`TAKANON_NAVLYS_WEB_SERVICE_HE.md`) must be
> reviewed and executed before an Israeli lawyer (עו"ד), whose signature
> verification is required for filing in any case.

**Article 1 — Name.** The name of the company is **NAVLYS WEB SERVICE LTD**
(in Hebrew: נבליס ווב סרוויס בע"מ), a private company limited by shares under
the Israeli Companies Law, 5759-1999.

**Article 2 — Objects (the SaaS / high-tech positioning).** The objects of the company are:
1. The **research and development (R&D)** of software, artificial-intelligence
   systems, algorithms and digital technologies;
2. The development, operation and provision of **Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)**:
   applications accessible by subscription, online, on mobile and desktop,
   including services based on software and on voice;
3. The **creation, ownership and exploitation of intangible assets** (software,
   platforms, trademarks, data, models) and the **grant of operating licenses**
   over such assets to third parties and partners;
4. The hosting, technical operation, maintenance and security of digital
   platforms for its own account and for third parties;
5. The marketing of subscriptions, educational and informational content and
   related digital services;
6. Any activity ancillary or conducive to the above objects, in Israel and abroad.

**Article 3 — Liability.** The liability of the shareholders is limited to the
unpaid amount, if any, on their shares.

**Article 4 — Share capital.** Authorized capital: 1,000,000 ordinary shares of
NIS 0.01 each. Issued upon incorporation: 100 shares to Bruno Mark Partouche (100 %).

**Article 5 — Sole shareholder and sole director.** The company may have a
single shareholder and a single director. First director:
**Bruno Mark Partouche**, with sole and unlimited signatory authority.

**Article 6 — Registered office.** The registered office is in Israel (address
to be completed upon filing).

**Article 7 — Intellectual property.** Any intangible asset developed by the
company, its officers, employees or contractors within the scope of its objects
is the **exclusive property of the company** — a condition of the Preferred
Technology Enterprise regime (the preferred asset must be owned by the Israeli
enterprise).

**Article 8 — Accounts.** The company shall keep proper books of account,
including **separate analytical tracking of R&D expenditure**.

**Article 9 — Dividends, transfer of shares, winding-up.** In accordance with
the Companies Law 5759-1999 and the resolutions of the sole shareholder.

---

*Note (not part of the Articles): the tax benefits do not derive from the
Articles themselves. The Articles open the door; eligibility for the Preferred
Technology Enterprise regime (12 % corporate tax, or 7.5 % in Development
Zone A) is established by the company's actual activity and bookkeeping — R&D
expenditure ≥ 7 % of revenue (3-year average), IP held by the company,
qualifying SaaS/licensing revenue, documented R&D headcount — as validated
annually with the accountant before the Israeli Tax Authority.*
