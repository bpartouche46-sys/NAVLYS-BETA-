## Champ : Business description (texte EN à coller)
```
NAVLYS is an editorial digital publisher producing pedagogical content
about personal finance and investment. The business model includes:

1. A monthly/annual subscription (NAVLYS NEXT GEN INVEST, 49€/month or
   490€/year) granting access to educational articles, financial
   calculators, and a private community.

2. One-time digital deliverables (NAVBIO Life: 19-199€) — personal-finance
   biographies in digital format.

3. NAVLYS.IO Phase 0 micro-products (9-19€) — early access to financial
   simulation tools.

NAVLYS does NOT provide investment advice, brokerage services, portfolio
management, nor any regulated financial intermediation. The merchant is
NOT registered as IOBSP, CIF, or ORIAS. Content is strictly editorial
and educational — comparable to Capital, Investir.fr, or financial
newsletters such as Snowball or Finary's editorial layer.

Customers are individuals interested in financial literacy. No
brokerage, no mandate, no client portfolio is operated by NAVLYS.

The website includes editorial affiliate links to regulated brokers and
neo-banks (publisher CPA model — same as YouTube finance creators or
blog editors). NAVLYS does not collect, hold, transmit or manage client
funds for any third party.
```
